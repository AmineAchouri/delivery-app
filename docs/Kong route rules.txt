Kong route rules (conceptual):

Create services/routes for:
/platform/* → platform service (no X-Tenant-ID check)
/auth/, /menus/, /offers/, /orders/, etc. → tenant service (require X-Tenant-ID)
Plugins:
jwt: enabled on both, different audiences/scopes
request-transformer: validate/passthrough X-Tenant-ID
rate-limiting: stricter on /auth/*
acl or custom policy to check feature flags (via upstream)
